Research Interests

My research interests are computer vision, remote sensing and robotics. I am interested in multimodal data fusion that integrates sensing data from different domains. I am also interested in developing robots that enable autonomous decision-making in diverse environments, particularly those devoid of Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) signals, spanning both indoor and outdoor settings. I am currently working on a task called Cross-View Geo-Localization, which involves matching satellite images with ground images.
Keywords: Systems Engineering, Satellite System Design, Space Habitat Design
